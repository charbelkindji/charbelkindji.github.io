I am a third-year PhD student in University of Rennes working on deep generative models with applications on tabular data. I am doing a CIFRE between Orange Labs in Lannion and IRISA in Rennes. My PhD is jointly supervised by Elisa Fromont (IRISA), Tanguy Urvoy (Orange) and Lina Maria Rojas Barahona (Orange).

I obtained my master’s degree in Big Data Decision Making and Machine Learning (now named “DAta and Business Intelligence” - DABI) from the same university.

I completed an internship at INRIA (2020) in the DRUID team, where I worked on studying the evolution of science through large graph exploration and natural language processing. I was supervised by Mickaël Foursov and Zoltan Miklos.
